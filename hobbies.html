Hiking

He likes hiking because it offers many benefits, ranging from physical exercise we get when out on the trail, to emotional or mental relief that connect with the nature. Hiking is an aerobic exercise so that it can help to improve the physical health. Moreover, getting out the indoor space to connect with the nature offers the mental health benefits. Hiking can provide the relief for depression, anxiety and stress. In addition, hiking can make us happier. It offers a good distraction that can help away from our everyday life by being connect with nature.
